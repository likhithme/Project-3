Welcome to Groundwater Conservation Game

"Learn while you play" is one of the most effective ways of teaching! This web-based game is designed to help students, youth, and water enthusiasts learn the importance of groundwater conservation in a fun and interactive way.

Through various scenarios like droughts, rainfall surplus, and contamination, you'll make decisions to conserve water resources. Earn points, compete with others, and become a water-smart champion!
